About Me

Hello, I'm Delia, an illustrative artist with a passion for portraying the human form and captivating everyday life stories in digital comic books and depictions.
My artistic identity is rooted in dark aesthetics and romantic themes. I am also focused on self-care and confidence.
As an artist, I tend to be transparent in my artworks to cultivate a connection with the audience.
Alongside my artistic practices, I enjoy facilitating creative art sessions with people, engaging in mindful art projects that allow me to learn more about inclusiveness in art and create meaningful artworks.
Through these interactions, I aim to hold a safe space where stories can be expressed and celebrated.
Having grown up in the landscapes of southern Italy, surrounded by sea and mountains, I am fascinated by local cultures, legends and myths that are hidden in villages.
I firmly believe in preserving and sharing those stories and in their importance for passing from wisdom to future generations.
